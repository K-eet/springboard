# PRD.md (Product Requirements Document)

## Overview

**Project Name:** Springboard Malaysia Corporate Website
**Project Type:** Static marketing website
**Client:** Springboard (consulting and ventures firm)
**Location:** Malaysia
**Timeline:** Prototype phase

## Purpose

The website exists to pass background scrutiny. When corporate executives Google "Springboard" after a meeting, pitch, or referral, the site must:
1. Confirm Springboard is a legitimate, professional operation
2. Showcase the Silicon Foundry (Kearney company) partnership
3. Display leadership and advisory team credentials
4. Clearly communicate the consulting + ventures business model
5. Provide a contact pathway

**This is NOT:**
- A lead generation funnel
- A startup accelerator site targeting founders
- A content marketing platform
- An e-commerce or SaaS product

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Corporate executives in Malaysia
- Innovation leads
- CTOs / Chief Digital Officers
- Procurement / vendor evaluation teams
- C-suite decision makers

**Psychographic profile:**
- Risk-averse, needs proof of credibility
- Will verify claims (LinkedIn, partner websites)
- Values professionalism over flash
- Skeptical of new/unknown vendors
- Looking for reasons to trust, not reasons to buy

## Success Criteria

1. A corporate executive landing on the site thinks "these people are legitimate"
2. Silicon Foundry partnership is immediately visible
3. Team credentials are verifiable (LinkedIn links work)
4. Site loads fast, works on mobile, looks professional
5. No "template" or "amateur" signals

## Features (In Scope)

### F1: Hero Section
- Large headline in Quintessential font (placeholder text acceptable)
- Subheadline referencing Silicon Foundry partnership (Instrument Serif)
- Single CTA button ("Get in Touch")
- Full viewport height
- Mellow light background (#FAF9F6), dark text

### F2: About/Value Proposition Section
- 2-3 sentence company description
- What Springboard does at high level
- Placeholder copy acceptable

### F3: Services Section
- Two offerings displayed as cards or columns:
  - Consulting
  - Ventures
- Each has: title, icon, 3-4 sentence description
- Placeholder copy acceptable

### F4: Partners Section
- Section title: "Strategic Partners" or "Our Network"
- Silicon Foundry logo placeholder (grey rectangle with text)
- Space for 3-6 future partner logos
- 1-2 sentence partnership description

### F5: Team Section
- Section title: "Leadership"
- CEO card (prominent):
  - Circular headshot placeholder
  - Name placeholder
  - Title: "Chief Executive Officer"
  - 2-3 line bio placeholder
  - LinkedIn icon/link
- Advisors subsection:
  - Grid of 4 advisor cards (smaller than CEO)
  - Each: circular headshot placeholder, name, title/affiliation, 1-line bio
  - LinkedIn optional

### F6: Contact Section
- Section title: "Let's Talk" or "Get in Touch"
- Simple contact form: Name, Email, Message, Submit button
- Alternative: mailto link
- Location text: "Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia"

### F7: Navigation
- Fixed/sticky header
- Logo on left (placeholder)
- Nav links: About, Services, Partners, Team, Contact
- All links are anchor links to page sections
- Mobile: hamburger menu

### F8: Footer
- Logo placeholder
- Copyright: "Â© 2025 Springboard. All rights reserved."
- Links: Privacy Policy, Terms (placeholder hrefs)
- LinkedIn icon

## Features (Out of Scope)

- Blog / News section
- Case studies
- Pricing page
- Client testimonials (none exist yet)
- Video embeds
- Animations beyond subtle hover states
- Newsletter signup
- Chatbot / live chat
- Multi-language support
- CMS integration
- Backend / database
- Authentication

## Non-Goals

- Do not generate leads directly
- Do not target startup founders
- Do not include any "accelerator" language
- Do not use playful/startup aesthetics
- Do not add features not listed above
